About me

Want to know more about me? :3

More that what I can do, I wanted to focus this part on who I am, because not even I am prepared for myself. Being brought up under unconventional circumstances, and probably by being an oddball by nature, I ended up with a very unorthodox way of processing information. Positives? I get to very weird (and sometimes insightful) thoughts. Negatives? The information I can best internalize is quite far from social interactions, despite my many efforts (and successes) in learning how to better interact with people.

At the core, I think that is my best quality: the capability of adding an extra flavour to projects I'm passionate about. Others things to note about me is that I'm generally chill and quite, have enough sense of responsibility to work hard even on things that I don't particularly enjoy, am honest to the bone, and I try to take care of the people around me. Because why wouldn't I. Oh, and sometimes I get too invested about some topics and end up dragging people into hours long rabbit holes ;P

What else I can say? I like the aesthetic of human anatomy, especially hands (#NotKira). If I see any cute animal I will start laughing uncontrollably. I literally compile information on everything that comes across as slightly interesting. Very fond of manga-anime's expressive potential. I write a lot about myself and the constants I see in my life. I can't properly sing, but I put enough passion in it that people don't notice. Despite being a designer, I'm trying my hand at music and drawing. And I suck at visual composition (if you couldn't tell by this website :').

That's about it, at least at a superficial level. I hope to have piqued your interest so that we may meet in the future to uncover life's horizons >:3
